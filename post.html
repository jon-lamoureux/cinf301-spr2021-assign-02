Reported Hours of Sleep

by Jonathan, 2/8/2021

46% of Americans aged 18 to 29 years old report getting 6 or less hours of sleep, due to a variety of different reasons. That age drastically decreases at the age 65+, but still remains high at 33%.

Age Sleep six hours or more Sleep seven hours or more
18 to 29 years old 46% 54%
30 to 49 years old 44% 56%
50 to 64 years old 38% 62%
65+ years old 33% 67%

Source: Gallup, 2013
